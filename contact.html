Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some questions our clients frequently ask.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes. We recommend booking an appointment to ensure that we can provide you with the best possible service and dedicated attention.

How long does wig revamp take?

The duration depends on the condition of the wig and the services required, but most wig revamps are completed within one to three days.

Can I book through WhatsApp?

Absolutely. You can contact us through WhatsApp to make bookings, ask questions, or request consultations.

Do you offer home services?

Yes. Selected services can be provided at your location depending on availability and the nature of the service required.
