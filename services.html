Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the questions our clients ask before booking an appointment.

How long does wig revamp or styling take?

Depending on the condition of the wig and the service required, appointments usually take between 1 and 3 hours.

Do I need to book an appointment?

Yes. Booking ahead allows us to prepare for your visit and give you the personalized attention you deserve.

How often should I treat or revamp my wigs?

We recommend professional treatment every 4–8 weeks, depending on how frequently the wig is worn.

Can damaged hair or wigs be restored?

In many cases, yes. Our restoration and silk infusion treatments are designed to revive damaged wigs and improve their appearance and longevity.
